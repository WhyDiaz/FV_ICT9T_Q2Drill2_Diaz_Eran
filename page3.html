About us


O.B. Montessori Center, Inc. (OBMC) is a progressive pre-k to 12 co-educational school in the Philippines that offers a viable alternative to international schools. Established in 1987 by Dr. Olive B. Montilla,

OBMC is dedicated to providing quality education based on the Montessori philosophy, which emphasizes self-directed learning, hands-on activities, and collaborative play.

The school aims to nurture well-rounded individuals who are not only academically competent but also socially responsible and globally aware. OBMC's mission is to transform students into self-developing and productive individuals who can succeed, direct, and influence the future of an ever-changing world.

With a strong focus on character development, critical thinking, and creativity, OBMC prepares its students to become future leaders and innovators in various fields. The school's vibrant community and diverse programs make it a unique place for children to grow and develop.
